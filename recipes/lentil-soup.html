Lentil soup

Description

A traditional recipe for lentil soup.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Onion
  • 1 slice of pork belly
  • 500g lentils (washed)
  • 2 litres of water
  • 2 bouillon cubes (with meat)
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 bunch of greens

Steps:

  1. Chop the onion
  2. Chop the pork belly
  3. Chop the bunch of greens
  4. Chop the potators
  5. Fry onion and pork belly in an open pressure cooker
  6. Add water, lentils, bouillon cubes, potatoes and greens
  7. close pressure cooker and let it boil for 15 minutes

Add Maggi and vinegar as you want.
